# Code Of Conduct

cxxmcp is a technical project. Participation should stay focused, respectful,
and evidence-driven.

Expected behavior:

- discuss code, behavior, tests, and design tradeoffs directly;
- assume reviewers and contributors are trying to improve the SDK;
- keep disagreement specific and actionable;
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, and disclosure of
  private information.

Unacceptable behavior may lead to moderation, removal of comments, or blocking
from project spaces. Report conduct problems to the maintainer through GitHub.
